#+title: Axon

This are some sort of wires on the brain; they can vary in length, from a few
milimeters to lengths necessary to connect different regions of the brain.

The axon's job is to transmit electrical signals formed in the [[id:0ccb6464-f654-499f-aeb2-0f6cbd6a7b45][Neuron]] cell body
to the axon terminal (also called presynaptic terminals) towards target neurons.
