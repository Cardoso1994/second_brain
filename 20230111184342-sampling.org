#+title: sampling

In [[id:1b63d5d6-2956-436d-8e0e-9374ad160f44][Statistics]] and [[id:1ea3fed6-1ad4-42ae-970a-e2306f9b2396][Probability]] sampling referes to the process where we (or a
computer) randomly picks a value from a distribution (either from a curve or
from a histogram), i.e., we choose a random value from the probabilities
described by the histogram or curve.
